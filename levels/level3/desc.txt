Chocolate Cake

First, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and butter in a bowl for 3 minutes to create the cake batter.
Bake the cake batter in the oven for 15 minutes until you get a baked cake.
Let the baked cake cool in the fridge for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix cream and sugar in a bowl for 4 minutes to make frosting.
Finally move to the cutting board and spread the frosting over the cooled cake to finish the chocolate cake, this will take 2 minutes.
