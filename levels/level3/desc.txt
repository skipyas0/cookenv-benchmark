Dinner Combo: Schnitzel, Pancakes, Omelette, and Potato Salad
Crack the eggs into a stand mixer and beat briefly to make mixed eggs.
Melt butter in a pan for 5 minutes, then strain it to get purified butter.
In the stand mixer, combine flour, water, and yeast to form bread dough. Let it rest in a bowl for 12 minutes until it becomes risen dough.
Mix milk with the risen dough and bake in the oven for 4 minutes to make bread. Cut it into cubes, bake again for 6 minutes to dry, then blend into bread crumbs.
On a plate, coat chicken with mixed eggs, flour, and bread crumbs to prepare raw schnitzel. Fry it in a pan with purified butter for 4 minutes to make schnitzel.
Boil potatoes in water for 8 minutes until soft to get cooked potatoes.
Heat oil in a pan for 3 minutes.
Use the hot oil to cook mixed eggs in the pan for 2 minutes, forming an omelette.
In the stand mixer, combine eggs, flour, and milk to make pancake batter. Cook it in the pan with hot oil for 4 minutes per side to get pancakes.
Boil eggs in water for 10 minutes to make hard-boiled eggs. Separate the yolks in a bowl.
Blend oil and egg yolks for 4 minutes to make mayo.
In a bowl, mix cooked potatoes, hard-boiled eggs, and mayo to make potato salad.
Finally, arrange schnitzel, omelette, pancakes, and potato salad neatly on a serving tray to complete dinner.
